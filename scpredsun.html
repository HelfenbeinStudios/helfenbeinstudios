Games

SCP: Red Sun is a horror survival game based on the SCP Foundation wiki. It is inspired by the story of SCP-001, "When Day Breaks" , by S. D. Locke.

Included in the game are three gamemodes: Story Mode, Security Mode, and Class-D Mode.

STORY MODE

Follow the main character through hordes of wax zombies and into an SCP Foundation site, where there's been a breach in containment. Who knows who, or what, you will find?

SECURITY MODE

Monitor security cameras of the SCP Foundation site in the security room. SCP-001 has caused many deaths, leading to the lack of security in the facility. Strategically open and close doors to keep SCPs away from getting to you.

CLASS-D MODE

SCP-001 has caused a lack of security. SCP containments have opened, and you have to survive. Explore the facility, and try to find survivors.
