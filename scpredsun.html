SCP: Red Sun is a horror survival game based on the SCP Foundation wiki. It is inspired by the story of SCP-001, "When Day Breaks", by S. D. Locke. Follow the main character through hordes of wax zombies and into an SCP Foundation site, where there's been a breach in containment. Who knows who, or what, you will find?
